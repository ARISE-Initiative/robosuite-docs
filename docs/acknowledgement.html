robosuite is built on the MuJoCo engine with the Python interfaces provided by mujoco-py. We would like to thank members of the Stanford People, AI, & Robots (PAIR) Group for their support and feedback to this project. In particular, the following people have made their contributions in different stages of this project:

  • Jiren Zhu, Joan Creus-Costa (robosuite v0.3)

  • Jim (Linxi) Fan, Zihua Liu, Orien Zeng, Anchit Gupta (Surreal experiments)

  • Michelle Lee, Rachel Gardner (controller implementations)

  • Danfei Xu (placement initializer)

  • Andrew Kondrich, Jonathan Booher (domain randomization)

  • Albert Tung (demonstration collection)

We wholeheartedly welcome the community to contribute to our project through issues and pull requests. New contributors will be added to the list above.
